1. Bring a saucepan (large enough to hold the eggs in
    a single layer) of water to a boil over medium-high heat.
  2. Using a slotted spoon, gently lower the cold
    eggs into the water one at a time.
  3. Cook for exactly 6½ minutes, adjusting the heat
    as necessary to maintain a gentle boil.
  4. Meanwhile, fill a bowl with cold water and some ice
    cubes. When the eggs are done cooking, transfer them
    to the water and chill until just slightly warm, about 2 minutes.
  5. If you don’t egg cups but would like to serve
    the soft-boiled eggs this way, you can also use shot glasses. Alternatively, the eggs can be peeled and sliced in half.

Soft Boiled Eggs


Serves: 4
Time: Cooks in 2 minutes
